## Why this site exists

The forum has generated important work, but information is currently scattered across NGO pages, reports, and campaign updates. This site brings the core story into one multilingual, public-facing portal.

## What this platform should do

1. Present a clear picture of health inequality indicators in East Jerusalem.
2. Publish advocacy updates and policy asks in three languages.
3. Offer a stable reference point for journalists, researchers, and donors.
4. Make resources easier to access for local residents and partner organizations.

## Core sections

- Home: mission, latest indicators, and major calls to action.
- About: timeline, governance, and coalition model.
- Data and Advocacy: policy briefs, letters, legal milestones, and recommendations.
